Final Fantasy RPG - Boot Camp Homework

Select Your Character

  • Select an opponent to fight.
  • Press the attack button to attack. Each subsequent attack increases your attack power.
  • If the enemy is still alive, they will initiate a counter attack.
  • After killing an opponent, select a new one. The game ends when you have defeated all three opponents.
  • Note: Each character has different stats. Your goal is to identify and kill the weaker characters while building your attack power.
